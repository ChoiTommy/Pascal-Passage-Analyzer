Once upon a time, there is a boy, called Hou Kai Syun. One day, he found a big banana in a forest. He decided to peel it off and suck it.
'Wait a moment, you can't suck it,' said the mysterious man. The man stopped him from doing that ero thing. 'You should lick it and move it in and out of your mouth!' The man suggested.
From then on, Hou Kai Syun (a.k.a. Master of Banana) becomes the most popular man in the world - the planet Mars.
